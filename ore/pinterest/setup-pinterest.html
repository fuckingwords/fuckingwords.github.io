Setup Pinterest Account (pinterest)

The following are steps to create a Pinterest Business account and how to setup the account.

Account Creation

  1. Go to https://www.pinterest.com/business/create.
  2. Go through the sign up process.
  3. ALWAYS select United States.

Account Setup

  1. Change your name and Pinterest URL. (If necessary)
  2. Start with 3 boards that represent your niche.
